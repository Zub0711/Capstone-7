What is fishing?

So what is fishing?, for those of you who dont know fishing is the art of catching fish. Humans have been engaged in fishing since ancient times by developing varies types of methods my personal favourite being by rod and reel.Fishing can be done in a diverse number of locations ranging from Alpine lakes,forest streams,ranging rivers ,Crushing surf and even deep isolated parts of the great ocean. It all depends what species of fish is on your Hit-list.

What attracted me to fishing?

When I was younger I spent most of my spare time playing video games. Looking outside my room window I which the days go by as I played my Ps2 and I thought it would be nice to get out so I did. In my quest to find a outdoor recreational activity I had my first experince fishing on the peir in Kalk bay in Cape Town.Standing watching the ocean with the smelling refreshing fragrances of the sea and having the gusts of sea air lightly batter my body it really awakens your senses. Even though I didn't catch anything that day I was Hooked.

What I like about fishing

One of Favourite things about fishing would be the peace. Its so peaceful and relaxing to get out in nature to cast your line in the water and just chill and wait for the fish to start biting. Another thing I like about fishing would be the variety wether it be salt-water or fresh-water environments or different types of tackle to the many types of species to be caught fishing just never stops giving in terms of the variety if things it offers. My final point that I would like to add would be affordablity. Fishing is fairly affordable which means that almost anyone can have fish no matter what type of lifestyle you come from fishing is for All.

A wise fisherman once said, "Fishing is a passion that can never be explained."
